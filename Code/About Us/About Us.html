Welcome to ERamen!

At ERamen, we are passionate about bringing the authentic taste of Japanese ramen to the world. As a leading ramen manufacturer, we are dedicated to crafting high-quality and delicious ramen noodles that capture the essence of this beloved dish.

History

ERamen Shop, founded in 1910 in Tokyo by Masato Suzuki, is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. In 2015, they opened their first international branch in Indonesia, adapting their recipes to suit local tastes while maintaining authenticity. Since then, ERamen Shop has expanded to various cities worldwide, captivating ramen enthusiasts with their flavorful bowls while preserving their century-old heritage.

Vision

Eramen's vision is to be the leading global provider of authentic and high-quality ramen, bringing the flavors and essence of Asian cuisine to people all over the world. Eramen aims to create a memorable dining experience that captures the true spirit of traditional ramen while embracing innovation and culinary creativity.

Mission

Eramen's mission is to craft and serve exceptional ramen bowls that showcase the artistry of Asian flavors, using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques. The company is dedicated to providing a warm and welcoming environment where customers can savor the authentic taste of ramen, created with passion and attention to detail.
